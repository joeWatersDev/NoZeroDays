# Privacy Policy — No Zero Days

**Last updated: March 19, 2026**

## Overview

No Zero Days is a personal habit tracking app. This policy describes how the app handles your data.

## Data Collection

No Zero Days does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no internet connection, no user accounts, no analytics, and no third-party SDKs.

## Data Storage

All habit data (your habit names and daily completion records) is stored locally on your device using Android's built-in database. This data never leaves your device.

## Data Deletion

Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.
